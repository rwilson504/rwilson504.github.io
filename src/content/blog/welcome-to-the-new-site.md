After more than fifteen years on Blogger, I'm moving this site to a custom
static build on GitHub Pages. Same domain (`www.richardawilson.com`), same
posts (working on it), same focus on Power Platform with a growing AI
section — just easier to write, easier to integrate with the tools I
actually use, and zero comment spam.

If you bookmarked an old `/YYYY/MM/slug.html` URL: it still works. Every
legacy URL forwards to its new home.
